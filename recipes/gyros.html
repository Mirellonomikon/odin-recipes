Gyros

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita bread
  • Tzatziki sauce
  • Sliced tomatoes, onions, and lettuce for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Add the chicken slices and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the marinated chicken until fully cooked and slightly charred, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Warm the pita bread in a dry skillet or oven.
  4. Assemble the gyros by placing cooked chicken on the pita bread. Top with tzatziki sauce, sliced tomatoes, onions, and lettuce.
  5. Fold the pita around the fillings and serve immediately.
