Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer 3 lasagna noodles, half of the ricotta mixture, half of the ground beef, and a third of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, ending with noodles and sauce on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil, add remaining mozzarella cheese, and bake for an additional 15 minutes.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
