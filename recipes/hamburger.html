Hamburger

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  4. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly browned.
  5. Assemble the burgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom buns. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments as desired. Top with the other half of the bun.
  6. Serve immediately with your favorite side dishes.
